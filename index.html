Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about GlucoBuddy and diabetes management.

How accurate is the AI food recognition?

Our AI food recognition system achieves 95% accuracy across thousands of food items. It's trained on extensive databases including McDonald's, local cuisines, and home-cooked meals. The system continuously learns and improves with user feedback.

Which glucose meters are compatible?

GlucoBuddy supports major glucose meter brands including Accu-Chek, OneTouch, FreeStyle Libre, Contour, and Dexcom. Our Bluetooth integration works with any meter that supports Bluetooth LE connectivity.

Is my health data secure and private?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption and comply with HIPAA regulations. Your data is stored securely and never shared with third parties without your explicit consent. You have full control over your data.

Can I use GlucoBuddy without a smartphone?

GlucoBuddy is designed as a mobile app for iOS and Android. However, you can access your data and reports through our web dashboard on any computer or tablet with internet access.

How does the BMI/BMR calculator work?

Our calculator uses medically-approved formulas (Mifflin-St Jeor for BMR) and provides personalized recommendations based on your age, gender, height, and weight. It integrates with your glucose data to provide comprehensive health insights.

What makes GlucoBuddy different from other apps?

GlucoBuddy combines AI food recognition, real Bluetooth glucose meter integration, and advanced analytics in one app. Our personalized insights and actionable health recommendations are based on your actual data patterns, not generic advice.
